Visualization 4: Line Panel - Average Billing Cost Over Time by Medical Condition (static)





Key Takeaways

  • Average billing costs show no trend for any condition during the 2019–2024 period, suggesting that annual inflation and COVID-19 disruptions did not produce a detectable shift.
  • Provider lines within each condition panel remain largely intertwined rather than separating into distinct tiers, reinforcing that insurer identity does not strongly predict whether a patient pays more or less over time.
  • Obesity and hypertension panels show slightly more fluctuation between providers year to year compared to conditions like cancer and diabetes, which maintain more stable provider trajectories, potentially reflecting greater variability in how chronic lifestyle-related conditions are billed.
