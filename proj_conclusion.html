Conclusion and Future Considerations




Conclusion of Findings: REVISE ACCORDING TO NEW PLOTS!!!

  • Billing varies significantly depending on provider and condition.
  • COVID-19 years show increased costs.
  • Hospitals charge differently for the same services.
  • Obesity tends to have the highest billing costs.
  • Pricing transparency remains a major issue.


Future Considerations

  • It's important to consider this research using organic data since synthetic data is often not reproducible using real-world data (it cannot capture outliers and complex, nonlinear relationships).
  • It would be worthwhile to depict any geospatial relationships in the data, which could potentially reflect the economy across different parts of the country.
  • Incorporating patient outcome data alongside billing information could help determine whether higher-cost providers and conditions actually correspond to better health outcomes
