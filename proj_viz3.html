Visualization 3: Box Plot — Billing Cost by Admission Type & Provider (interactive)





Key Takeaways

  • Billing amounts are broadly consistent across all five insurance providers, with medians clustering near $25,000 regardless of admission type, suggesting cost uniformity across insurers.
  • Admission type (elective, emergency, urgent) shows minimal difference in cost distribution within each provider, indicating that urgency of admission alone does not significantly drive billing variation.
  • Cigna's elective admissions carry a slightly higher median (~$26,500) compared to other providers, while UnitedHealthcare trends marginally lower overall. Click providers in the legend to isolate and compare individual insurers.
