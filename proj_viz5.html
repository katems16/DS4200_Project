Visualization 5: Heat Map - Average Billing Cost by Medical Condition and Insurance Provider (interactive)





Key Takeaways

  • The heatmap color is strikingly uniform across the entire 6×5 grid, with all condition–provider combinations averaging between roughly $24,800 and $26,100, indicating nearly equal billing costs.

  • Obesity paired with Blue Cross ($26,101) and Cigna ($26,117) represents the highest-billing cells, while cancer with UnitedHealthcare ($24,858) and Cigna with hypertension ($24,860) sit at the low end.
